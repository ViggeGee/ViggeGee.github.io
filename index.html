ABOUT ME

Hello!

I am a 24 year old Game Development student studying in Malmö University. I am in my third year at the Bachelor Program in Game Development. My game engine of choice is Unity but I am currently learning Unreal Engine as well. My primary languages are C# and C++ with some minor knowladge in Java. I have always been very interested in technology and I am constantly looking for more ways to deepen my knowledge. I believe that my passion and curiosity can be seen in the commitment I have made in school by being a teachers assistent for Object Oriented programming in C# Development, and by taking part in multiple Game Jam, as well as participating in multiple playtests for different companies. Do you want to know more? You can find my contact information at the bottom.
